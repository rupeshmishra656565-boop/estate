Welcome to Lokrakshak Real Property Center. Led by Dr. R.R. Mishra, we are a government-recognised property dealing firm dedicated to serving the Navsari district and beyond.

Dr. Mishra is not only a seasoned real estate professional but also a deeply committed social worker. He proudly holds the position of President: Environment and Human Rights National NGO, Gujarat, India. This commitment to human rights and the environment translates into how we do business — with strict ethics, complete transparency, and a focus on community development.

Our firm handles all categories of real estate — from agricultural and NA plots to luxury residential bungalows and commercial properties — always ensuring that every client's interest is fully protected throughout the legal process.
